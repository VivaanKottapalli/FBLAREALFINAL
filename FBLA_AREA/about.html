About Us

Welcome to Suburban Biking, a company run and managed by 3 passionate high school entrepreneurs. Our mission is to give back to the community and motivate others to create new products. Our trio of high school students is dedicated to creating high-quality helmets at affordable prices. All of our products are made in the USA, and we are constantly working to add new lines of products to our store. We are passionate about giving back to the community and have made it a priority to donate a portion of our profits to local charities. Our goal is to not only provide top-notch products, but also make a positive impact in the lives of those around us. At Suburban Biking, we believe that anyone can make a difference in the world and we are proud to be leading by example. We invite you to join us on our journey to change the world, one helmet at a time.
